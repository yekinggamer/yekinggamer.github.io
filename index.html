Instructions

The Personalize Homepage page has two regions: A list of homepages region and a tiles region. You can add, delete, or reorder homepages or tiles on this page. The Personalize Dashboard page has just one region: a tiles region. You can add, delete, or reorder tiles on this page. To reorder homepages, tab to place focus on the source homepage that you want to move. Press Ctrl+M to grab it. Tab again to find and place focus on the target homepage. Press Ctrl+Alt+M to drop the source homepage at the location of the target homepage. To reorder tiles, tab to place focus on the source tile that you want to move. Press Ctrl+M to grab it. Tab again to find and place focus on the target tile. Press Ctrl+Alt+M to drop the source tile at the location of the target tile. Prior to dropping the source object, when moving to the target location by pressing the Tab key places the source after the target; when moving by pressing Shift+Tab places the source before the target.
